OUR STORY

Who we are

We're a top-rated international charity organization With your help, we're working to end global poverty. We're focused on impact. We work in four key areas to help kids break the cycle of poverty: health, education, empowerment and employment.

What we do

We’re a charity that takes a bold, effective approach to solving poverty through four key areas: Health – Education – Empowerment – Employment. Children access these important programs and services through our amazing community centers.

How we help

Your gifts guarantee sponsored children receive the support that dramatically changes lives. This includes equipping, staffing and operating 67 community centers in the 10 countries we serve, providing thousands with a safe place to access crucial benefits and programs.
